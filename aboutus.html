ABOUT US

RupayBachao is a dynamic digital trading platform that empowers businesses across industries to buy and sell goods and services within a secure, member-only ecosystem—without cash transactions.

Members trade seamlessly using RFT (Rupee Free Token), a cashless exchange mechanism that enables businesses to increase sales, optimize inventory, and preserve cash flow. All transactions are digitally recorded, ensuring transparency, security, and convenience through the RupayBachao mobile application.

Promoted by the reputed Gold Souk Group, a four-decade-old diversified conglomerate with interests in real estate, entertainment, and hospitality, RupayBachao brings credibility, strength, and vision to the digital barter ecosystem.

  • Conserve cash through RFT-based trading
